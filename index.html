What do we do

We supply delicious freshest fruits such as mandarins, oranges, pomegranates, lemons, kiwis and olives. Our seasonal products are handpicked, sorted, packed and delivered to your home within the next 24. All you have to do is to make your pick and order it from the comfort of your home.

What we offer

Orange

Commonly called sweet orange, they grow on orange trees (Citrus x sinensis). A healthy source of fiber, vitamin C, thiamine, folate, and antioxidants.

Season: middle of November - middle of January

Lemon

Meyer lemon, a cross between a citron and a mandarin/pomelo hybrid. Good for baked goods, sauces, salad dressings, marinades, drinks and desserts. High in vitamin C.

Season: start of December - end of May

Mandarin

Belonging to the Citrus genus. Theu are oblong, their peel is deep-orange and leathery, sweet and juicy. Multiple types such as Citrus Unshiu, Saigon, and Chahara.

Season: middle of September - middle of December

Pomegranates

Pomegranates are round fruis with hard, shiny red-yellow skins, loaded with important nutrients such as fibre as well as vitamins C, K, B, calcium, potassium and iron.

Season: middle of September - middle of November

Kiwi

The kiwifruit, or Chinese gooseberry, originally grew wild in China, are nutrient-densed food - rich in nutrients and low in calorie. Good for healthful skin and better sleep.

Season: start of October - end of December

Olive Oil

Large ammount of antioxidants, anti-inflammatory properties, may help preventing strokes, protective against hearth disease, antibacterial properties, and so much more.

Season: All Year Around
